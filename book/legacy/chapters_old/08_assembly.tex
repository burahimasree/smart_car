\chapter{Assembly Guide}

\section{Wiring Manifest}
\subsection{Power (The Critical Path)}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Connect **Li-Ion Battery +** to L298N 12V Input.
    \item Connect **Li-Ion Battery -** to L298N GND.
    \item Connect **L298N GND** to **ESP32 GND** AND **Raspberry Pi GND** (Common Ground).
    \item Power the ESP32 via its 5V/VIN pin or USB (driven by Battery or separate Regulator).
    \item Power the Pi 4 via Official USB-C Supply.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Data Links}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{UART}:
    \begin{itemize}
        \item Pi GPIO 14 (TX) $\rightarrow$ ESP32 GPIO 16 (RX).
        \item Pi GPIO 15 (RX) $\rightarrow$ ESP32 GPIO 17 (TX).
    \end{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Sensors}: Connect Ultrasonic Trig/Echo pairs as defined in Firmware.
\end{itemize}

\section{Cooling Assembly}
1. Apply thermal paste to Pi 4 CPU.
2. Mount the **Metal Case**.
3. Connect Dual Fans to Pi 5V and GND GPIO pins.
\textbf{Test}: Run `stress -c 4` and verify temp stays $< 60^\circ$C.

\section{Software Deployment}
